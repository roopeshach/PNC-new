About PN Campus

Situated in the beautiful city of Pokhara, PN Campus is the leading teaching and research campus in the western region of Nepal. With over 50 years of its history, the campus is one of the oldest and largest campuses of Tribhuvan University and is dedicated to finding solutions to challenges and preparing students for leadership in a complex world.
